Mission

AI in the Community!

The USC Viterbi AI Community Project aims to introduce artificial intelligence and emerging technologies to broader communities—especially students and communities that traditionally have less exposure to engineering—through hands-on workshops, demonstrations, and educational outreach. The project focuses on making AI understandable, approachable, and useful by showing how it works in real-world applications while encouraging more diverse participation in engineering and computer science.

AI Workshops

Introductory sessions that translate machine learning concepts into tactile, beginner-friendly activities.

Live Demonstrations

Interactive showcases that connect AI perception, automation, and systems control with everyday applications.

Community Education

Outreach events designed for families, K-12 students, and local organizations interested in technology for social good.

AI Literacy

Clear explanations of how AI works, where it appears in daily life, and how communities can shape its future responsibly.

A program model centered on curiosity, access, and community impact.
